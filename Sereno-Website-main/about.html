Our Mission

Sereno With You began with a simple belief: everyone deserves a calm, supportive space for their mental health. Our founders saw how challenging it can be to ask for help, to develop new habits, and to maintain wellbeing in a fast‑paced world. Sereno With You brings together daily mood tracking, friendly AI coaching, expert sessions, and a caring community, all designed to meet you where you are.

We work with certified coaches, therapists, and practitioners around the world to provide trusted guidance. Our AI tools make it easy to check in with yourself, learn new skills, and build resilience. And our events and group sessions foster connection so you never feel alone on your journey.

Our team is dedicated to scientific rigor, privacy, and inclusivity. Sereno With You is built in India for the world, and we’re just getting started. Join us as we reshape mental wellness—one gentle step at a time.
