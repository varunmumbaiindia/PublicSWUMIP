Why attend wellness events?

Webinars and workshops offer live interaction that pre‑recorded content cannot match. You can ask questions, share experiences and feel the energy of a supportive community. Our events range from free introductory sessions to in‑depth masterclasses. Whether you want to manage anxiety, improve relationships or learn mindfulness basics, there’s something for everyone.

To stay updated on future events, follow us on social media or join our newsletter. We’re continually adding new topics and collaborating with experts across India and globally.
