Book Certified Wellness Coaches

Sometimes you need more than a self‑help app. Working one‑on‑one with a trained professional can accelerate your growth, provide accountability and help you navigate complex emotions. Sereno With You makes it simple to find and book certified coaches and therapists, whether you’re dealing with anxiety, relationship challenges or career stress.

How our marketplace works

Sereno’s expert marketplace is built on trust and transparency. Every coach and therapist on our platform undergoes a verification process to ensure they hold relevant qualifications and adhere to ethical guidelines. Once verified, experts can list group sessions, workshops and private consultations along with their availability and rates. Users browse profiles, read reviews and choose the person who best matches their needs. Payments are processed securely, and sessions are conducted via video call within the app.

To support accessibility, we allow experts to offer sliding scale pricing and free community sessions. We also ensure data privacy — session notes are confidential and never shared without your consent.

Choosing the right coach

Finding a good match can feel daunting. Consider the following tips:

  • Identify your goals: Are you seeking help with anxiety, career decisions, relationship issues or general wellbeing? Clarifying your focus will narrow your search.
  • Read bios and specialties: Experts on Sereno share their qualifications, modalities and areas of expertise. Look for someone who aligns with your needs and values.
  • Check availability: Ensure the coach offers session times that work with your schedule. Many provide weekend and evening slots.
  • Start with a trial: Some coaches offer shorter introductory sessions or free consultations. Use this to assess rapport before committing.

Ultimately, trust your intuition. You should feel comfortable, respected and heard during sessions. If it doesn’t feel like a good fit, it’s okay to explore other professionals.
