One‑liner

Sereno With You is a mental health and wellness platform that combines mood tracking, an empathetic AI coach and access to verified experts to support your wellbeing every day.

100‑word description

Sereno With You is building a gentle space where mental health care is accessible, inclusive and stigma‑free. Our free app lets you track your emotions, chat with an AI coach for personalized coping strategies and attend live events on topics like anxiety, relationships and productivity. For deeper support, our marketplace connects you with licensed psychologists, certified life coaches and mindfulness trainers. Designed in India with global best practices, Sereno With You is on a mission to make mental wellness part of everyday life.
